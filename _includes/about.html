Background

What initiated this project?

  • 2020

    The Nail Art Beginnings

    My mom and I, we used to go out and have our own nail dates, but the pandemic hit and we had to stay in, so I started to order basic supplies to practice and then take care of my mom's nails every 3 weeks.

  • 2021

    A Business was Born

    When I got back to school as a sophomore, I started my nails career while still taking 17 credits (do not recommend). Starting from my close friends, my customers circle widen to 3-4 nail sets per week.

  • 2022

    Common Problem Repeats

    The more sets of nails I did, the more I realize it takes a lot of time to come up with designs, and it's even harder for my customers to visualize the nail designs in their mind. So the idea of an app to visualize designs were thought of.

  • 2023

    Creating Solution

    After giving this idea a lot of thoughts, I decided to take action. Hence, this website was created, designs are being created, and an app will be created not for just me but for other nail techs out there having the same problem as well!
