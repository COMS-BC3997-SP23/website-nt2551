I'm Chloe and I do nails for fun now (used to be professionally). As I spend so much time doing and designing nails, it's very often to hate the vision that I have for a set of nails after it's done. Similarly, my friends and customers felt the same way after they have their set done because so many things could go wrong (same shape different length look differently on different hands, just to start with). I needed a solution to be more sustainable to pursue my love for nail art, and here it is, nailARt.
